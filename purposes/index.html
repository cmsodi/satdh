    Services

    This purpose covers the provision of services using satellites and other space-based systems, including satellite communication and navigation, space tourism and transportation, and space debris management.

    Exploitation

    This purpose involves the commercial use of space resources and energy generation in space, including space manufacturing, resource extraction from celestial bodies, and the generation of renewable energy from space.

    Research (R&D)

    The Research purpose deals with the scientific exploration of space and the development of new technologies, encompassing space science and technology, earth science, and the study of human behavior in space.

    Strategy

    The Strategy purpose addresses the legal, policy, and diplomatic aspects of space activities, including the development of international agreements and policies, and the regulation of space activities for sustainable and peaceful use.
